About Me

Allow me to introduce myself briefly. After graduating secondary school in London back in 2007, I took a break from my secular education until 2016. I then did an Access Course in Biology and Chemistry followed by an undergraduate degree in Food and Human Nutrition in Newcastle University where I was privileged to learn from great professors and teachers.

I always had a keen interest in anatomy and physiology, as well as how eating healthy was conducive to a long and healthy life. My bachelor's degree took this interest to the next level and helped me sift through the mass of knowledge out there and distinguish science from its distant cousin pseudo-science.

I have a full time job as a book-keepr, leaving some time in the evenings and weekends to work with clients, create nutrition plans and calculating nutrition labels. My hobbies include reading, cycling, music and spending time with my lovely kids!
